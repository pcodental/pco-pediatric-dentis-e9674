Frequently Asked Questions About Invisalign

How much does Invisalign treatment typically cost at PCO Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics in New York, NY?
At our New York City office, Invisalign treatment generally ranges from $4,000 to $7,500, depending on the complexity and duration of your child’s case. We provide a personalized estimate after your initial consultation. We also accept most major dental insurances and offer flexible payment plans to help make treatment more affordable for families in NYC.
How long does Invisalign treatment usually take for kids and teens?
The average Invisalign treatment time for pediatric and adolescent patients at PCO Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics is 12 to 18 months. However, the exact timeline depends on how much tooth movement is needed and how consistently your child wears their aligners. We’ll review your child’s progress at regular visits every 6-8 weeks at our Manhattan office.
Is PCO Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics licensed to provide Invisalign for children in New York State?
Yes, all of our orthodontists are fully licensed by the State of New York and have specialized training in pediatric orthodontics and Invisalign treatment. We are also a certified Invisalign provider, with additional experience working with children and teens to ensure safe, effective results.
What is the process for getting started with Invisalign at your NYC office?
To begin Invisalign treatment at PCO Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics, we’ll first schedule a comprehensive orthodontic evaluation, including digital scans and photos. If Invisalign is right for your child, we’ll create a custom treatment plan and order the aligners. Once they arrive (usually within 2-3 weeks), we’ll fit the first set and review care instructions. Follow-up visits will be scheduled every 6-8 weeks so we can track progress and provide new aligners.
Does PCO Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics guarantee results with Invisalign for children and teens?
While we cannot guarantee specific outcomes, we are committed to providing the best possible results for every patient. Our orthodontists closely monitor progress and make adjustments as needed throughout treatment. If your child’s teeth do not move as planned, we may recommend additional aligners at no extra cost to help ensure a healthy, beautiful smile.
