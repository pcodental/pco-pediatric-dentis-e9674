Frequently Asked Questions About Tooth Extraction for Kids

How much does a tooth extraction for kids typically cost at PCO Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics in New York, NY?

Please contact our office for a personalized estimate based on your child’s needs.
How long does the tooth extraction process take for kids, and what is the typical recovery time?
A typical tooth extraction appointment at our New York office lasts about 30 to 60 minutes, including preparation, the procedure, and aftercare instructions. Most children recover from a simple extraction within 1-3 days, while more complex extractions may require up to a week of gentle care. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and are available to answer any questions during your child's recovery.
Are your pediatric dentists and orthodontists licensed and experienced in performing tooth extractions for children?
Yes, all of our pediatric dentists and orthodontists at PCO Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics are fully licensed in New York State and have extensive training in pediatric dental care, including tooth extractions. Our team is experienced in providing gentle, age-appropriate care and uses child-friendly techniques to ensure your child’s comfort and safety during the procedure.
What is the process for a child’s tooth extraction at your New York office, and how do you keep kids comfortable?
Before the extraction, we’ll review your child’s dental and medical history, take necessary X-rays, and discuss the procedure with you and your child. On the day of the extraction, we use local anesthesia and can offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas) if needed. Our team explains each step in a kid-friendly way, provides distractions, and uses gentle techniques to minimize anxiety. Afterward, we give you clear aftercare instructions and are available for any follow-up questions.
Do you offer any guarantees or follow-up care after my child’s tooth extraction?
At PCO Pediatric Dentistry & Orthodontics, we are committed to your child’s well-being. We offer follow-up appointments at no additional charge if there are any concerns after the extraction.
